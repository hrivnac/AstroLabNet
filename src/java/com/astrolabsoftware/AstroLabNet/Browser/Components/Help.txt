<h1>This is the AstroLabNet Help !</h1>

<h2>How to see available Data ?</h2>
<ol>
<li>Select <b>Data</b>, <b>Data Source</b> or <b>Data Channel</b> on the top/left tree.</li>
<li>A new pane with more information and relations will appear in the central pane.</li>
</ol>

<h2>How to execute an Action ?</h2>
<ol>
<li>Select one or several parts of <b>Data</b>, <b>Data Source</b> or <b>Data Channel</b> on the top/left tree.</li>
<li>Select <b>Action</b> on the top/left tree.</li>
<li>A form will appear in the central pane - fill missing fields.</li>
<li>Send the command - it will appear as a <b>Job</b> and/or <b>Session</b>.</li>
</ol>
